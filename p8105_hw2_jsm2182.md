## Clean 2018 Precipitation Data

``` r
precip_2018_data = 
  read_excel ("./data/Trash-Wheel-Collection-Totals-7-2020-2.xlsx", 
  sheet = "2018 Precipitation", skip = 1) %>% 
  janitor::clean_names() %>%
  mutate(year = 2018) %>% 
  drop_na()
```
